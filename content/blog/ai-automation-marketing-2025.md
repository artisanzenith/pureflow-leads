AI can amplify output — when used with guardrails. Below are workflows you can deploy this week to increase conversions and save time.

## 1. Short-form ad creative generator

Feed top-performing headlines and ad copy into a prompt that produces 20 variants. A/B test the best-performing ones and iterate.

## 2. Lead triage and summary

Auto-summarize form submissions and score leads by keywords and behavior, then route top leads to humans for quick follow-up.

## 3. Email personalization at scale

Use templated prompts to insert company-specific facts and pain points into outreach while keeping the core offer constant.

## 4. Content briefs for writers

Generate outlines with headings, keyword targets, and internal linking suggestions to speed up human writing.

Start with one workflow, measure time saved, and validate conversion lift before scaling.
