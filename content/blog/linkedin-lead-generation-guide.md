LinkedIn remains the highest-intent B2B channel when used with restraint. Agencies that win treat it as a relationship channel, not a bulk DM machine.

## Optimize profiles for buyers

Founder and AE profiles should speak to client outcomes, not internal awards. Featured section showcases proof.

## Content before cold outreach

Three valuable posts per week warm the network. Comments on ICP posts build familiarity before the invite.

## Connection request discipline

Personalize one line referencing their content or company news. Cap daily invites to protect account health.

## Conversation, not pitch

First messages ask questions or offer resources. Pitch only after engagement signals interest.

## Track leading indicators

Measure conversations started and calls booked—not connections accepted. Quality connections beat vanity metrics.
