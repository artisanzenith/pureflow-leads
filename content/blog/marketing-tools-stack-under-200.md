Bootstrapped teams don't need 40 tools—they need the right eight wired together cleanly.

## Analytics and tagging

Start with GA4 and a tag manager. Free, powerful, and essential for any paid or organic strategy.

## Email and CRM

Combine a free-tier CRM with an email platform that scales with list size. Integrate on day one to avoid data silos.

## Design and landing pages

Use one builder for landing pages and lead magnets. Consistency beats switching tools mid-campaign.

## Scheduling and social

Buffer or native scheduling keeps presence steady without hiring a full-time social manager early.

## Where to invest next

Once revenue supports it, add call tracking, heatmaps, or a dedicated SEO tool—one at a time, tied to a specific hypothesis.
