Choosing a CRM shouldn't require a six-month implementation project. For US small businesses, the right platform balances simplicity, integrations, and price per seat.

## What small teams actually need

Pipeline stages, contact history, email sync, mobile access, and basic reporting cover 80% of use cases. Avoid paying for enterprise features you'll never configure.

## HubSpot CRM

Best for teams wanting free core CRM with optional marketing add-ons. Strong ecosystem; costs rise quickly as you add hubs.

## Pipedrive

Visual pipeline focus suits sales-heavy service businesses. Fast onboarding, fewer marketing features out of the box.

## Zoho CRM

Budget-friendly with deep customization. Steeper learning curve but strong value for price-sensitive operators.

## How to decide in one week

Import 50 real contacts, build your actual pipeline stages, and run one week of live deals through each finalist. Usability under pressure reveals the winner.

## Our recommendation

There's no universal best CRM—only the best fit for your sales motion. Document requirements first, trial second, contract last.
