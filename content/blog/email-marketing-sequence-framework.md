Your welcome sequence sets the tone for every future email. Get it right and subscribers become advocates. Get it wrong and unsubscribes spike before email two.

## Email 1: Deliver the promise

Send what they signed up for immediately. No sales pitch. Confirm expectations and set reply-friendly tone.

## Email 2: Your origin story

Share why you built the business and who you serve. Stories build trust faster than feature lists.

## Email 3: Quick win

One actionable tactic they can implement today. Proof you deliver value before asking for anything.

## Email 4: Social proof

Case study, testimonial, or metric. Make results specific and relevant to your core reader.

## Email 5: Soft CTA

Invite them to your best resource, book a call, or try a tool. One clear next step—no menu of options.

## Testing and optimization

A/B test subject lines on email one only until open rates stabilize. Then test CTA placement on email five. Small lists need patience; significance takes volume.
