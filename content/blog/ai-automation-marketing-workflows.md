AI hype is everywhere; useful workflows are harder to find. These seven automations deliver consistent time savings for US marketing teams we work with.

## Content repurposing pipeline

Turn one webinar into blog drafts, social posts, and email snippets with human editing at each stage. Never publish raw AI output.

## Lead scoring enrichment

Use AI to summarize firmographic and behavioral signals before SDR review. Humans make the call; machines prep the brief.

## Competitive monitoring

Automated alerts on competitor pricing, features, and messaging changes keep positioning current without manual stalking.

## Compliance guardrails

Build prompt templates with legal-approved disclaimers for regulated industries. Document what AI can and cannot draft.

## Start small, measure hours saved

Deploy one workflow per month. Track hours reclaimed and redeploy talent to strategy and creative work—that's where ROI compounds.
