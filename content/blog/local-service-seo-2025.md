Local search still drives the most consistent bookings for service businesses. These tactics focus on fast visibility gains you can apply today.

## 1. Optimized Google Business Profile

Complete every field, add 5–10 photos, post weekly updates, and collect reviews. Reply to reviews professionally and promptly.

## 2. Service-area landing pages

Create pages for each service + locality (e.g., "plumber in Seattle"). Keep content focused on buyer intent and include local schema.

## 3. Structured data for local businesses

Add `LocalBusiness` schema with `address`, `telephone`, `openingHours`, and services. This helps SERPs display rich results.

## 4. Citation consistency

Ensure NAP (name, address, phone) is identical across directories. Fix duplicates and incorrect listings.

## 5. Fast content wins

Publish 2–3 FAQ-style posts answering real customer questions (pricing, time to service, guarantees). These rank quickly.

Implement these this week: update your GBP and publish one local landing page — traffic typically increases within days for focused queries.
